What We Offer

Sailing for Everyone

⛵

RYA Level 1: Introduction to Sailing

Master the fundamentals of rigging, sailing, and water safety. A full day on the water with expert instruction. Perfect for complete beginners. 1 day, all materials provided.

🎯

RYA Level 2: Sailing Essentials

Build on Level 1 with advanced boat handling, navigation, and seamanship. Develop confidence in varied conditions. Prerequisites: Level 1 or equivalent experience. 2 days, progressive coaching.

🌊

Taster Sessions

Discover sailing in just 2 hours. No experience needed. Feel the thrill of the water, meet our instructors, and see if sailing is for you. Small groups, personal attention, all equipment provided.

⚓

RYA Competent Crew

The stepping stone to day-skipper level. Learn professional crew skills, including rope work, sail trim, and emergency procedures. 5 days, ideal for sailors moving toward independence. Adults and younger sailors welcome.

🧭

Bespoke Coaching & Refresher Days

Custom sessions tailored to your goals—whether brushing up rusty skills, perfecting technique, or preparing for qualifications. 1-to-1 or small groups. Choose your dates and focus areas.

Why Choose Us

Experienced Instruction on England's Largest Lake

With over 15 years of RYA teaching experience, our instructors deliver professional, personalized coaching in a safe, welcoming environment. Lake Windermere's sheltered bays and consistent winds make it the ideal classroom for learning sailing. Whether you're taking your first steps on the water or refining advanced techniques, we'll guide you every nautical mile.

About Me

Experienced, Friendly, Passionate

I'm a dedicated piano teacher based in Chester with over 15 years of experience. I believe every student—whether a curious five-year-old, a determined teenager, or a confident adult—deserves a supportive, encouraging environment to flourish. My lessons are tailored to your pace, goals, and learning style. Whether you're preparing for ABRSM or Trinity exams, exploring music for joy, or rediscovering the piano after years away, I'm here to guide you.
